# Releasing the perimeter checklist

Owner: Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

Scope: Perimeter release checklist after flames have subsided.

## Before burn

- [ ] Has access to all maintained checklists relevant for this role

## During burn

- [ ] No role-specific items for this phase.

## After burn

### The perimeter is OK to release when

- [ ] Structure has collapsed or stabilized
- [ ] No unsafe leaning material remains
- [ ] Hot spots are controlled
- [ ] No active flying ember risk
- [ ] Fire lanes are no longer needed
- [ ] Fire spinning equipment is clear
- [ ] Extinguishing crew agrees
- [ ] Area is safe for limited entry
