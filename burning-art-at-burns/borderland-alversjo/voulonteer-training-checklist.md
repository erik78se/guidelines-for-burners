# Volunteer training checklist

Owner: Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

Scope: Training checklist for perimeter volunteers before a large burn.

## Before burn

- [ ] Has access to all maintained checklists relevant for this role
- [ ] Knows time to arrive for duty
- [ ] Knows time and place to arrive for duty before the burn
- [ ] Knows time and place to arrive for duty during the burn
- [ ] Knows time and place to arrive for duty after the burn
- [ ] Knows who is who in the burn organization
- [ ] Knows what happens when during the burn timeline
- [ ] Received basic training in how to deal with a burning person
- [ ] Knows where fire materials are and how to use them
- [ ] Knows who is the "Burn Lead"
- [ ] Knows that they are allowed to take decisions to make the burn safe
- [ ] Knows that they are supposed to be sober to take responsible decisions
- [ ] Knows what to do if there is an uncontrolled fire breakout
- [ ] Knows where the exit lanes are and how to get people there if needed
- [ ] Knows that fire lanes must stay clear
- [ ] Knows how to separate an audience from a crew member (FAST vests)
- [ ] Knows they should be visibly identifiable, for example with FAST vests or another clear marker
- [ ] Knows safety crew should be visibly different from ordinary participants, performers and artists
- [ ] Knows that it is very important to have fun even if safety is important too
- [ ] Knows that it is common that people try to run towards the fire
- [ ] Knows to identify runners as early as possible
- [ ] Knows how to stop runners
- [ ] Knows they should not do anything heroic
- [ ] Knows they should be spaced approximately 5 m apart during perimeter duty
- [ ] Knows that they have authority to expel people that violate the perimeter
- [ ] Knows that they should at all times stay safe
- [ ] Knows who is the Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead
- [ ] Knows that there are some special rules for the fire art spinners
- [ ] Knows who is the Fire Artists / Fire Spinner Lead
- [ ] Knows the conditions when releasing the perimeter is OK

## During burn

- [ ] No role-specific items for this phase.

## After burn

- [ ] No role-specific items for this phase.
