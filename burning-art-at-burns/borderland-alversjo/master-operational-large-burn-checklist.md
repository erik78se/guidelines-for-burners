# Master operational large burn checklist

Owner: Burn Lead

Scope: Cross-role operational checklist for planning, approving, running and closing a large public burn.

## Before burn

### Burn Lead

- [ ] Confirmed every role has access to the maintained checklists relevant for their responsibilities
- [ ] Burn proposed before placement is finalized
- [ ] Burn Lead named
- [ ] Timeline(s) approved
- [ ] No active fire ban checked
- [ ] Final go/no-go checklist ready

### Borderland Representative

- [ ] Fire department / neighbor communication plan

### Safety Lead

- [ ] Structure specifications known, including height
- [ ] Collapse behavior considered
- [ ] Burn radius mapped
- [ ] Forest distance mapped
- [ ] Audience area mapped
- [ ] Fire lanes mapped
- [ ] Emergency access mapped
- [ ] Water access or pump plan mapped
- [ ] Forest risk acceptable

### Medics Lead

- [ ] Medics Lead named

### Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

- [ ] Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead named
- [ ] Volunteer training session scheduled
- [ ] Active volunteer count calculated
- [ ] Double volunteer recruitment target set
- [ ] FAST vests or equivalent ready

### Fire Artists / Fire Spinner Lead

- [ ] Fire Artists / Fire Spinner Lead named
- [ ] Performers registered in advance
- [ ] Fire spinner briefing completed
- [ ] Dipping stations placed safely relative to wind
- [ ] Extinguishing stations marked
- [ ] Unbriefed spontaneous performers excluded

### Ignition Team

- [ ] Ignition crew briefed

### LNT Lead

- [ ] LNT Lead named
- [ ] Materials and metal fasteners cleanup plan ready

## During burn

### Burn Lead

- [ ] Weather acceptable
- [ ] Wind direction and strength acceptable
- [ ] Final all-clear given

### Safety Lead

- [ ] Safety briefing completed
- [ ] Water, extinguishers and fire blankets ready
- [ ] Forest risk monitored

### Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

- [ ] Perimeter established and staffed
- [ ] Fire lanes clear
- [ ] Audience informed

### Fire Artists / Fire Spinner Lead

- [ ] Fire spinning completed or safely separated

### Ignition Team

- [ ] Ignition only after all-clear

## After burn

### Burn Lead

- [ ] Perimeter held until officially released

### Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

- [ ] Perimeter release checklist complete

### Fire Artists / Fire Spinner Lead

- [ ] Fire spinning equipment clear

### LNT Lead

- [ ] Hot spots controlled
- [ ] Ash, nails, screws, wire and debris removed
- [ ] Burn scar inspected and restored if needed
