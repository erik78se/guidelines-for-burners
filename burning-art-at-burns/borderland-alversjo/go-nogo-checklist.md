# Go/no-go checklist

Owner: Burn Lead

Scope: Final decision checklist before ignition.

## Before burn

- [ ] Has access to all maintained checklists relevant for this role
- [ ] Fire ban checked
- [ ] Weather checked
- [ ] Wind direction checked
- [ ] Wind strength checked
- [ ] Forest risk reviewed
- [ ] Perimeter radius marked
- [ ] Fire lanes marked and clear
- [ ] Perimeter volunteers in position
- [ ] Volunteers identifiable
- [ ] Fire spinning complete or separated
- [ ] Dipping stations safe and downwind risk removed
- [ ] Extinguishing stations marked
- [ ] Water / pump / extinguishers ready
- [ ] Medics Lead ready
- [ ] Comms tested
- [ ] Ignition crew briefed
- [ ] Audience informed
- [ ] Neighbor / fire department plan handled
- [ ] Cleanup crew confirmed
- [ ] Burn Lead gives final all-clear
