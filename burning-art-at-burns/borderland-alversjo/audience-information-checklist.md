# Audience information checklist

Owner: Volunteer / Perimeter Crew Lead

Scope: Audience information checklist before a large burn.

## Before burn

- [ ] Has access to all maintained checklists relevant for this role
- [ ] When the burn is happening
- [ ] Where to stand
- [ ] Where not to stand
- [ ] Where the exits are
- [ ] Who the safety volunteers are
- [ ] That fire lanes must stay clear
- [ ] That the burn may be cancelled
- [ ] That crossing the perimeter may stop the burn
- [ ] When fire spinning happens
- [ ] When the burn area is safe to enter afterward
